## Overview

Virtual Patriot is a retrospective portfolio entry for an early cybersecurity and systems-visualization concept associated with Clint Hill. Owner-provided historical context describes the original effort as a Raspberry Pi–based exploration, developed around 2015, intended to make potentially suspicious network activity easier to understand through visual models and geographic or organizational context.

The materials preserved here are historical photographed diagrams and presentation artifacts. They are included as evidence of the concept-development process; they are **not** presented as current application screens, a functioning security dashboard, or proof of live threat detection.
